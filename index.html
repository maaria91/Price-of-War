The Price of War

This interactive visualizations was a project done by Maaria Assami as
part of the Design Studio 2 class in the Information Design and
Visualization MFA program during the Spring 2016 semester under the
instruction of Perdo Cruz. It captures the grim history surrounding the
different interstate wars that happened between 1823 and 2003, and
the price that was payed by the bloodshed that accompanied those wars.

After selecting the desired timeframe from the timeline, you will see the
number of wars that were fought during that period. The bloodier the
time period, the more the drop fills up.

Data source: www.correlatesofwar.org
